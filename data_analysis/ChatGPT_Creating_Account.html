How to Create a ChatGPT Account

Step 1: Go to the OpenAI Website

Navigate to the OpenAI website at https://openai.com

Step 2: Click on 'Sign Up'

Find and click on the 'Sign Up' button on the top right of the webpage.

Step 3: Use your school email address, Google, Microsoft, or Apple account

Step 4: Enter Your Information

Step 5: Start Using ChatGPT

Once your account is confirmed, you can start using ChatGPT at https://chat.openai.com.
